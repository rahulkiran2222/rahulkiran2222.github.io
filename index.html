Three Lenses, One Gap: A Unified Theoretical Account of Hallucination in Large Language Models

arXiv preprint · cs.AI, cs.CL · forthcoming

Surveys three independent theoretical traditions explaining LLM hallucination and introduces the grounding deficit Δ(M,F) = (δS, δC, δE) as a unifying construct with a composite irreducibility argument and proposed proxy metrics.
